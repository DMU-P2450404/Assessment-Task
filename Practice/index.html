What is K-pop?

The simplest way to put it is pop music that is in Korean.
What makes it so different to English pop? To answer that, I would personally say that it is the dedication that the artists have for their craft. I udnerstand that all artists are decided to their work. But K-pop takes it to the next level.

What are K-dramas?

Just like K-pop is pop in Korean, K-dramas are dramas that are in Korean.
I wouldn't say that they are really any different to English dramas as they are just as dynamtic and over the top sometimes.
